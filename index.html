Adventure Players choose a bomber hero of their own to participate in each level, at each level the player needs to destroy all the monsters to pass the level. Breaking blocks and killing monsters also have a chance to drop BCOINs.
 Arena Battle Manage Heroes Players choose a hero to join a bomb battle with many other players. Users have to pay a certain amount of tokens to participate in battle mode, the final winner will receive the entire loser’s token amount. Bomber Hero will also lose a certain amount of energy when participating in battle mode. Users can acquire new heroes via rescue missions. In some maps, there is a rare chance users meet a prison block which locked a bomber hero. After accomplishing the rescue mission, you will receive a damaged hero, which needs to be recovered a while before it can go wild again.
